About Us

We're The Nations Leading Seller of Foreclosed Homes

CastleRock REO is one of the largest purchasers of distressed foreclosed homes in the US, buying in excess of 14,000 homes over the last few years. We buy previously foreclosed houses in bulk, which allows us to sell our properties at a lower price than you’ll find otherwise. We purchase residential properties at a large discount, due to the massive number of foreclosures that have taken place over the past few years. Banks are literally selling houses for pennies on the dollar, and as a result of our long-standing relationships with these lending institutions, we’re able to purchase our properties at a large discount, passing massive savings on to our buyers.
